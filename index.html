We Are MVR Studio

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking MVR Studio.

You can request a custom quote by filling out our booking form or clicking the WhatsApp button. Let us know your event date, location, and requirements, and we'll send you a customized quotation within 2 hours.
The number of crew members depends entirely on the size of your event. For smaller ceremonies (like Haldi or Mehndi), 1-2 photographers are perfect. For grand weddings and receptions, we recommend 3-4 crew members (including drone pilots and cinematic videographers) to capture every angle.
We deliver edited photos within 7–15 working days. Priority delivery is available on request. Full cinematic wedding films take 15–21 days for professional editing and grading.
